Set-up in 6 steps

  1. Open the app with “Launch the web app”.
  2. On the welcome screen, set your reminder time and (optionally) enable notifications.
  3. Install it to your home screen / desktop (see “Get it on your device”).
  4. Do the Daily dose each day — it ticks off practice and grows your streak.
  5. Tick off Curriculum, add Projects & Goals, reflect in the Journal.
  6. Back up anytime via Settings → Export JSON (and Import to move devices).

Login: Pathway is local-first — no sign-in required, and nothing is uploaded. To sync across devices later, it can be connected to a free backend (Supabase).
