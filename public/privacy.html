Pathway — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

Pathway is a personal coding-learning tracker. It is designed to run entirely on your own device.

What we collect

Nothing. Pathway has no accounts, no analytics, and no server that receives your data. Everything you enter — practice logs, journal entries, projects, goals, quiz results and settings — is stored only in your browser/app on your device using localStorage.

How your data is used

Your data is used only to display your progress back to you within the app. It never leaves your device unless you choose to export it (Settings → Export JSON) and share that file yourself.

Third-party services

  • The app is served as static files from a web host (e.g. GitHub Pages). Standard web-server request logs may apply, per that host's own policy.
  • The Python exercises load the open-source Pyodide runtime from a public CDN the first time you run Python. No personal data is sent.
  • No advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics are used.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent anywhere.

Children

Pathway collects no personal data from anyone, including children.

Deleting your data

Use Settings → Reset everything in the app, or clear the site's data in your browser/app settings. This permanently removes all stored data from your device.
